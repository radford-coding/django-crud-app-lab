About the Berry Picker

I like long romantic walks down the U-Pick rows. My first Django project includes:

  • CRUD on selected berry varieties
  • CRUD on berry farms
  • CR on berry-picking excursions
  • Authentication and Authorization

Extra features beyond the scope of the assignment:

  • Adding in the Farm model with many-to-many relationships and full CRUD
  • Dynamically choosing shades of green based on the properties of each Farm class instance - I wrote a custom template function
  • Associating the BERRY_CHOICES within the Berry model, for auto-selecting a matching image
